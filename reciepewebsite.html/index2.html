Gulab jamun

Ingredients

  • bread
  • sugar
  • oil

    • Reciepe

  • Mix milk powder, flour, ghee, and milk to form a soft dough. Shape into small balls.
  • Heat oil, fry the balls till golden, and then soak them in sugar syrup (sugar dissolved in water).
  • Let Gulab Jamuns soak for a few hours, and serve warm for a delightful Indian dessert.

    barfi

    Ingredients

  • bread
  • sugar
  • oil

    • Reciepe

  • Combine condensed milk and your choice of nuts or flavorings like cardamom powder in a non-stick pan.
  • Cook the mixture on low heat, stirring continuously until it thickens and leaves the sides of the pan.
  • Pour the mixture into a greased tray, smooth it out, and let it cool. Once set, cut it into desired shapes, and your Barfi is ready to enjoy.
